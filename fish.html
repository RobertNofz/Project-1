Species of Fish

What have we explored?

Today we have explored most of freshwater, but believe it or not, we have only explored five to seven percent of the ocean! Even though that is true, we know about 1.3 million species of fish, which makes us believe that there is a potential of half a million of fish species we can discover since we know only five to seven percent of the ocean species.
